A concept e-commerce moible app to accompany Eunie Kim's senior thesis, Way Bag.

As a twenty-something growing up in the digital age with smartphones, I have noticed that we as a society have shifted from shopping in stores to the web and now on our mobile phones and tablets. I want to create a functional and effective application which would bring ease to online shopping without the frustration that users tend to feel.

For this project, I created my mockups in Sketch and worked out my prototype in Origami. I also did some product photography using a Nikon D600.
